1 Introduction

Welcome to the LuLu Warehouse Clearance application ("App"), operated by Lulu Group International LLC ("we," "us," or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled responsibly and in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

This Privacy Policy describes the types of information we may collect from you or that you may provide when you download, install, register with, access, or use the LuLu Warehouse Clearance mobile application, and our practices for collecting, using, maintaining, protecting, and disclosing that information.

By downloading, accessing, or using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the App.

2 Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our services to you. The categories of information we may collect include:

2.1 Personal Information

Information that identifies you as an individual, which you provide directly to us when creating an account, placing an order, or contacting customer support:

  • Full name and preferred display name
  • Email address
  • Telephone or mobile phone number
  • Residential or delivery address
  • Date of birth (where applicable for age-restricted products)
  • Account login credentials (username and encrypted password)
2.2 Device Information

Technical information collected automatically when you access the App:

  • Device type, model, and manufacturer
  • Operating system type and version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • Internet Protocol (IP) address
  • Mobile network and carrier information
  • Language and regional settings
2.3 Usage Data

Information about how you interact with and use the App:

  • Pages and screens viewed within the App
  • Features accessed and actions taken
  • Products browsed, searched for, or added to your cart
  • Time and duration of your visits
  • Referral source (how you found the App)
  • Error logs and performance data
2.4 Payment Information

When you make a purchase through the App, payment processing is handled entirely by trusted third-party payment processors. We do not directly collect, store, or have access to your full credit card numbers, debit card numbers, or bank account details. We may receive and retain:

  • Transaction reference numbers and order identifiers
  • Confirmation of payment status (completed, pending, or failed)
  • Last four digits of the payment card used (for your reference)
  • Billing address (if different from delivery address)

3 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect about you for the following purposes:

3.1 Order Fulfillment and Delivery

To process, confirm, and deliver your orders; to send order status updates and delivery notifications; and to facilitate returns, exchanges, and refunds where applicable.

3.2 App Experience Improvement

To personalize and enhance your browsing experience; to display products and offers relevant to your interests and preferences; to analyze usage trends and optimize App performance, layout, and functionality.

3.3 Promotional Communications

To send you information about exclusive clearance deals, flash sales, new product arrivals, and promotional events. You may opt out of marketing communications at any time by using the unsubscribe link in any promotional email, adjusting your notification settings in the App, or contacting us directly.

Your Choice: We will never send promotional messages without your consent. You can manage your communication preferences at any time through the App settings or by contacting our support team.

3.4 Customer Support

To respond to your inquiries, complaints, and requests; to provide technical assistance; and to resolve disputes related to your transactions or account.

3.5 Fraud Prevention and Security

To detect, prevent, and respond to fraud, unauthorized transactions, security threats, and other potentially prohibited or illegal activities; to verify user identity where necessary; and to protect the rights, property, and safety of our users and the public.

4 Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell your personal data. We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their own marketing purposes. Your data is not a commodity.

We may share your personal information only in the following limited circumstances and only to the extent necessary:

Delivery and Logistics Partners

We share your name, delivery address, and phone number with our trusted delivery and logistics partners solely for the purpose of delivering your orders. These partners are contractually obligated to protect your information.

Payment Processors

Transaction data is shared with our certified PCI-DSS compliant payment processors to facilitate secure payment transactions. These processors operate under their own privacy policies and are prohibited from using your data for any other purpose.

Analytics Providers

We use analytics services to understand how users interact with the App. These providers receive aggregated and anonymized usage data to help us improve App performance, identify issues, and enhance user experience. Individual user identification through analytics data is not possible.

Legal and Regulatory Compliance

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities, including courts, regulatory agencies, or law enforcement bodies. We may also disclose information when we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a government request.

5 Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement a range of industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect it against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our security practices include:

  • Transport Layer Security (TLS/SSL) encryption for all data transmitted between your device and our servers
  • AES-256 encryption for sensitive data at rest within our storage systems
  • Secure, access-controlled server infrastructure hosted in certified data centers
  • Regular security audits, vulnerability assessments, and penetration testing
  • Strict access controls limiting employee access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Secure password hashing algorithms for account credentials

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we are committed to continually improving our security measures.

6 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The App may use cookies, local storage, and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. These technologies help us:

  • Remember your preferences and login session across visits
  • Understand which features are most popular and how the App is used
  • Monitor App performance, detect errors, and measure load times
  • Analyze aggregated traffic patterns to improve infrastructure

We use analytics tools to collect anonymized data about App usage patterns. This data does not personally identify you and is used solely for the purpose of improving the App experience. You may control certain tracking technologies through your device settings.

7 Children's Privacy

The LuLu Warehouse Clearance App is not directed to, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from, children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in the relevant jurisdiction). If you are under 13 years of age, please do not use the App or provide any personal information to us.

If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal information from a child under 13 without verification of parental consent, we will take immediate steps to delete that information from our records. If you believe that we may have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately using the details provided in Section 12.

8 Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location and applicable data protection laws, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right of Access: You may request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: You may request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
  • Right to Erasure: You may request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain legal exceptions.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You may request that we limit the processing of your personal information under certain circumstances.
  • Right to Data Portability: You may request to receive your personal information in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: You may object to the processing of your personal information for direct marketing purposes.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on your consent, you may withdraw that consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in Section 12 below. We will respond to your request within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before processing your request. There is no fee for exercising your rights, except in cases of manifestly unfounded or excessive requests.

Opt-Out of Marketing: You can opt out of promotional notifications at any time through your App settings, by using the unsubscribe mechanism in our emails, or by contacting us directly. Please note that opting out of marketing communications does not affect transactional messages related to your orders.

9 Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including to satisfy legal, accounting, or regulatory requirements. The retention periods depend on the type of data:

  • Account Information: Retained for the duration of your active account and for up to 24 months after account closure or last activity.
  • Transaction Records: Retained for a minimum of 6 years to comply with financial and tax regulations.
  • Usage and Analytics Data: Retained in anonymized form for up to 36 months for trend analysis and App improvement.
  • Customer Support Records: Retained for up to 24 months after resolution of the inquiry.
  • Marketing Preferences: Retained until you modify your preferences or request deletion.

When personal data is no longer needed for the purposes stated in this policy, we will securely delete or anonymize it. Anonymized data that cannot be linked back to you may be retained indefinitely for statistical analysis.

10 International Data Transfers

Lulu Group International LLC is headquartered in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. Your personal information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in countries other than the one in which it was collected, including the UAE and other jurisdictions where our service providers operate.

When we transfer personal data internationally, we ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information, in accordance with applicable data protection laws. These safeguards may include:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses approved by relevant data protection authorities
  • Adequacy decisions recognizing the data protection standards of the receiving country
  • Binding Corporate Rules within our group of companies
  • Your explicit consent, where required by law

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or other regions with data transfer regulations, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and equivalent local laws when transferring your data outside of your jurisdiction.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technologies, legal requirements, or other factors. When we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you through one or more of the following methods:

  • A push notification to your device (if you have enabled notifications)

The "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy will be revised whenever changes are made. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information. Your continued use of the App after the posting of changes constitutes your acceptance of such changes.
